Our Story

Not everyone on this bill played Lilith Fair.
They all deserved to.

Fairly Lilith brings the sound of 90s female-fronted pop and rock back to the stage — the radio anthems, the aching ballads, the songs that defined a decade of women who wrote their own rules. Some of these artists graced Sarah McLachlan's original stage. Others just as easily could have.

Either way, it's a fairly accurate night of music you already know every word to.
